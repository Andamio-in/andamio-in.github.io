Acerca de_

El Tren is a project that has two versions: a fixed-video piece and an expanded reading format. Its origin is the work through personal memories constructing a text that interacts with sound and landscapes. Once the text production has been finalized and the sound lines are proposed, the image was generated. In its expanded reading format, the elements are worked in real-time, so the image, the sound and the text interact with each other <br> The train is the reading of a story that begins with a vacation trip, making the image and sound wandering, allowing them to build their own narrative, but at the same time finding moments where everything seems to coincide; However, it continues to bifurcate as the narrative of the story does, that never specifies that it is the history of the train.
