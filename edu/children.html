About

Educational projects with children ground on audiovisual practices and their connection with collective storytelling and storysharing. Here, texts, sounds, and visuals come together to expand literary and poetic texts.
Children can see step by step the construction of each of the elements: the body as the main generator of sound, the text that is integrated, where the words also contain a sonic and visual layers, or everything that is around of us when you read.

Metodology

Practice-based, the children collectively experiment with these elements to produce something together. From this perspective, listening, moving, and doing is a continuum of activities carried out during the workshop so that the children build their own interpretations and appropriate what they consider significant. We end with the presentation of a reading accompanied by audiovisual production that expand the child's way of approaching the digital.
