Installation Arragements

The installation can be presented with two setup options:

Option 1

The installation space should be big enough for audience, and to place:

  • (1) a long table, of 2 meters by 1 meter, or 3 to 4 small tables
  • (2) three raspberry pi (we will provide/bring these)
  • (3) three electrical multi-outlets
  • (4) two electrical extension cords

We will need to place the projector stand in the ceiling, so we will need to make modifications to place them (it can be solved using two totems).

Visual part:

  • (5) 2 video projectors with VGA or HDMI cables.
  • (6) 2 totems, or tall structures, each one in each side of the table to place the projectors.

Sound part:

  • Stereo sound
  • (7) 4 speakers same model with long XLR cables and 8 TRS.
  • (8) 4 speakers’ stands; they can also be place on the floor.
  • (9) 1 Mixer and 1 Sound interface with at least 4 inputs and outputs
  • (10) 1 Speaker’s set, we will provide this
Option 2

The installation space should be big enough for audience, and to place:

  • (1) one computer that will have to stay for the exhibition,
  • (2) two electrical multi-outlets, and
  • (3) two electrical extension cords. We will need to place the projector stand in the ceiling, so we will need to make modifications to place them. (it can be solved using one totem).

Visual part:

  • (4) 1 video matrox with three VGA or HDMI outputs.
  • (5) 2 video projectors with VGA or HDMI cables (it depends of the matrox).

Sound part:

We have two arrangements, depending on the technical capacities of the festival/space. The easiest arrangement is stereo sound; the second one is using a quadrophonic sound system with:

  • (6) 4 speakers, same model with long XLR cables and 8 TRS.
  • (7) 4 speakers’ stands; they can also be place on the floor.
  • (8) 1 Mixer
  • (9) 2 Speaker’s set, we will provide these.
