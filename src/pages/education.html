About_

Andamio is a collaborative project that includes producers working with visuals, music, and text from different perspectives. Besides producing art, we have systematized our artistic methods by designing and facilitating a series of workshops where we share our experiences, both theoretical and practical.

All workshops focus on digital literacy and informal education: workshops, collaborative discussions, artistic encounters, etc. In these terms, we have facilitated workshops around electronic literature, creative coding, visual-music, audio-visual creation, among others. Working with analog, electrical and digital systems.

The following sections contain information about educational projects that we facilitated with kids and young adults. If you are interested in us facilitating a workshop, please contact our member Jessica Rodrguez (jac307@gmail.com). We offer workshops in both Spanish and English.
