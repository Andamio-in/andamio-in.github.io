About_

Educational projects with Young Adults (16-30 years old) focus on acquiring technical and theoretical technical skills for media art (E-Lit, Live Coding, Visual-Music, etc.) and storytelling production (Cinema, Expanded Poetry, Performance, etc).

Metodology

All activities, short and long, are practice-based. Both theory and practice are intertwined. We review the theoretical approach for concepts such as sound, music, audiovisual production, media, intermedia, multimedia, performance, literature, and speech, among others. Next, we collectively analyze existing artworks, connecting concepts with actual practices.
In parallel, we share our knowledge in specific software, as well as storytelling techniques (writing and readin-in-public). With this framework, attendees can create, or co-create (collaborate), experimenting with the information given, and connecting it with their own experiences and knowledge.
